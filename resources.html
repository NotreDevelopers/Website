  • Hopefully this can explain how to best use this webpage. Just keep in mind that THIS DOES NOT MAKE YOUR GAME. Its purpose is to give you the proper equipment you need, and beside that, you should spend little time on this page. Lastly, GIYF (Google Is Your Friend). Ask us anything, but Google more likely has the answer/resource you're looking for.

    There are three sections: First Steps, Tools, and Assets, Sites, Etc.

    • First Steps: When you have never used this page or practiced making a game before, this might be the best section to read first. How to Make Your First Game has a collection of articles laying out useful advice or steps and tools to use in making your first game, and Sortingh.at is a cool website that allows you to answer simple questions to determine which tools and assets collections might be useful for you.
    • Tools: Here is our recommended collection of tools for game development. Try to not get stuck on which tools to use; in the end, as long as it can make your game, it should work. Also, ask us if you need any help or clarification! To search, either use CTRL-F and keywords on this webpage, or go to the Google Sheets and do refined searching there (like Filters and such).
    • Assets, Sites, Etc.: This is the most important part of the page and the only reason you should continually revisit. This section contains as many general resources as we can fit into it, for as many styles/makes of games as possible. When you're making your game and need an asset or tutorial, hopefully we can offer something here. (Search like in Tools)
